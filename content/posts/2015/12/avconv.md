convert video to jpeg with good quality:
```bash
avconv -i input_video.mp4 -qmax 1 -qmin 1 images_%05d.jpg
```

crop video:
```bash
avconv -i input.avi -vf crop=<width>:<height>:<x>:<y> output_%05d.png
```
