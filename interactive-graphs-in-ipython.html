Interactive graphs in IPython

Date Sun 07 June 2015 Tags useful / ipython
There are at least two libraries for ipython able to plot graphs interactively and inline. 1) [mpld3 ](http://mpld3.github.io/) 2) [bokeh](http://bokeh.pydata.org/en/latest/) I have tested only mpld3. It looks awesome. It implements exactly the thing I missed without matlab. Inside the notebook I now have a possibility to zoom and move plots. Of course, I could do it using qt mode, but it not so nice and convenient. Demo of mpld3: [http://mpld3.github.io/_downloads/mpld3_demo.html](http://mpld3.github.io/_downloads/mpld3_demo.html) How I use it: 
fig, ax = plt.subplots(2, 2, figsize=(8, 8),sharex=True, sharey=True)
fig.subplots_adjust(hspace=0.0)

ax.flat[1].imshow(img[1])
